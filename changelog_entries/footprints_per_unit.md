### Units
   * Assigned a fitting movement footprint to every unit, so each now leaves an appropriate trail when moving (following up on the footprint rework in #10837).
     * Core units and mainline campaign units alike were given a `footprints=` value matching their form (e.g. flyers use bird_silhouette, mounts use hoof, spiders and woses use many_legged, snakes and swimmers use swirling_pattern).
     * The Walking Corpse, Soulless, and the Secrets of the Ancients corpses/soulless get a footprint per body type, matching the creature that was reanimated.
